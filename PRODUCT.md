# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
AI researchers, academic reviewers, and machine learning engineers in the streaming video generation domain. They are looking to evaluate the method's validity, understand its core mechanism, and decide whether to build upon or cite the work.

## Product Purpose
To showcase the "SlotMemory" research in a way that is highly impactful and easy to follow. The ultimate goal is to help other researchers find the work useful, thereby encouraging adoption, code usage, and increasing the paper's citations.

## Brand Personality
Engaging, academic, and grand. It should feel rigorous but not dry, utilizing a bright and memorable presentation that leaves a strong impression.

## Anti-references
- Monotonous, generic academic templates (e.g., causvid.github.io).
- Default "cookie-cutter" paper pages that look like dozens of others and fail to leave a lasting visual memory.
- Overly dark or drab color schemes (preference is for brighter, grander aesthetics, unlike Forcing-KV-Page's color scheme despite liking its grand feel).

## Design Principles
- **Grand but Grounded:** Balance an impressive, engaging visual presentation with clear, rigorous scientific communication.
- **Memorable Differentiation:** Break away from generic academic boilerplate. Use deliberate layout, bold typography, and brighter colors to stand out.
- **Path to Impact:** Ensure the core innovation (object-centric KV memory) is instantly legible and accessible, removing friction to reading the paper and citing the work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web best practices. No strict WCAG level or specialized accommodations are explicitly required, but semantic structure and legible contrast should be maintained naturally.
